About us

Our Story

We here at Learning Ladder aim to help the community by making education more fun. Its called gamification of learning, and it involves applying the principles of video games to studying.

Our Mission

Our mission is to invent a way of incorporating a narrative into your educational horizons including studies which involves learning via invented scenarios.

Our Method

ncorporating the elements of video games that make them so interesting, such as design, narrative, leaderboards and reward schemes, is a way to make studying more fun, increase your motivation and develop superior productivity.
